Accelerated product enhancements
through parallel execution

At Endeavor, I designed and managed enterprise systems that redefined how global teams collaborated. From fighter onboarding to royalty approvals, I built scalable structures that aligned nine international departments under one cohesive workflow.
Challenges
  • Fighter onboarding was fragmented, with inefficiencies from registration to drop-off.
  • Royalty & eApprovals system was outdated, limiting responsiveness to global product updates.
  • Ticket-based design requests across nine departments lacked prioritization, creating bottlenecks.
  • Team KRAs and KPIs were inconsistent, leading to uneven performance and friction.
Execution
  • Designed a fighter onboarding ERP ecosystem, integrating registration, compliance, and retention flows.
  • Led the global redesign of Royalty & eApprovals (IMG), making it modular and reactive for faster rollouts.
  • Built a streamlined ticket-based workflow, reducing approval cycles and aligning deliverables across regions.
  • Reframed KRA & KPI structures monthly, improving accountability and driving interdepartmental learning.
  • Partnered directly with Endeavor CIO Melanie Hildebrandt to deliver high-stakes internal and external design solutions.
Results
  • Reduced turnaround time for approvals across nine global departments.
  • Accelerated product enhancements by enabling parallel execution across teams.
  • Standardized performance measurement, boosting accountability and cohesion.
  • Delivered enterprise systems that became internal benchmarks for operational clarity.

Objective:

To design and implement enterprise systems that unified fragmented workflows across fighter onboarding, royalty approvals, and global design requests, creating a cohesive framework that aligned nine international departments, reduced turnaround time, and set internal benchmarks for operational clarity.

Scope

Partnered directly with C-level leadership and department heads to align enterprise systems with high-stakes operational goals.
